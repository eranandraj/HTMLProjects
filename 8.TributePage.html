Nikola Tesla

1856-1943

The man who electrified the world

About Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Key Achievements

  • Development of the AC electrical system
  • Invention of the Tesla coil
  • Contributions to wireless communication
  • Early work on X-rays and radio waves

Legacy

Nikola Tesla's work laid the foundation for many modern technologies. His visionary ideas continue to inspire scientists and engineers around the world.
