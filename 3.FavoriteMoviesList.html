My Top 5 Favorite Movies

Here is a list of my top 5 favorite movies that I enjoy watching again and again.

  • Inception (2010) - A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan that explores the world of dreams.
  • The Shawshank Redemption (1994) - A powerful drama about hope and friendship set in a prison.
  • The Dark Knight (2008) - A gripping superhero film featuring Batman's battle against the Joker.
  • Forrest Gump (1994) - A heartwarming story of a man's extraordinary life journey through historical events.
  • Interstellar (2014) - A visually stunning sci-fi epic that explores space travel and the survival of humanity.
